# Christopher Nolan

Visionary filmmaker known for ambitious, mind-bending narratives. His films push the boundaries of what cinema can be.

<!-- more -->

Director of *The Dark Knight Trilogy*, *Inception*, [[interstellar|*Interstellar*]], *Tenet*, and *Oppenheimer*. What I admire most is his commitment to practical effects, IMAX cinematography, and storytelling that respects the audience's intelligence.
