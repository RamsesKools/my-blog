# Dune by Frank Herbert

An epic science fiction novel that defined the genre. Rich worldbuilding, political intrigue, and a protagonist's journey that still feels relevant today.

<!-- more -->

The intricate details of the Fremen culture, the ecology of Arrakis, and the complex interplay of noble houses make this a masterclass in world-building.
